Common Questions

Straight answers to help you decide.

What is your staff-to-resident ratio?

We maintain a strict 1-to-3 ratio. This allows us to provide true personalized care, unlike large facilities where ratios can be 1-to-15.

How do you handle hurricanes?

We have a permanent, whole-house generator. Residents stay safe, cool (A/C), and comfortable in their own beds. We are fully stocked with hurricane supplies year-round.

Do you accept VA benefits?

Yes! We help families utilize the VA Aid & Attendance benefit. We also work with Long Term Care Insurance.
